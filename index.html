05 — About

Sound. Vision.
Motion.

Jeri Nova is a Las Vegas-based electronic music artist and producer whose sound traverses the outer edges of techno, ambient, and experimental electronic music — with an ear tuned to the cosmos and a heart locked to the grid.

Every live set is an immersive experience where visuals and sound collapse into a single unified force — leaving audiences suspended between rhythm and reverie. Whether it's a sweaty club basement or an open desert stage, Jeri Nova brings the frequency.
